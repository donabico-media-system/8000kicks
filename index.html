Detailed Performance Knowledge Base (FAQ)

Are 8000Kicks truly 100% waterproof, or simply water-resistant?

8000Kicks are fully 100% waterproof. They combine a specialized plant-based outer bio-shield coating with an embedded microscopic inner membrane layer[span_11](start_span)[span_11](end_span). This dual-layer system blocks exterior water from passing through while allowing interior vapor to escape, preventing any damp or humid environment around your foot.

How do hemp shoes react to mud and heavy dirt accumulation over time?

Industrial hemp has remarkable organic tensile strength and fiber durability. Unlike standard canvas cotton, which stretches and weakens when wet, hemp maintains its strong structural shape. Dried mud can be brushed off easily, and the hydrophobic bio-shield coating blocks deep dirt from staining the fabric matrix[span_12](start_span)[span_12](end_span).

What makes Bloom Foam insoles superior to standard athletic shoe cushioning?

The insoles are crafted from recycled toxic algae biomass harvested from compromised waterways. This prevents environmental pollution while providing a highly supportive, shock-absorbing cushion. It delivers lasting arch support and naturally controls moisture inside your shoe.

Can these hemp sneakers be machine washed safely?

We recommend spot cleaning with warm water and mild soap to maximize the lifespan of the waterproof coating. If machine washing is necessary, use a gentle cycle with cold water, remove the insoles, and let the shoes air dry naturally. Never place them in a mechanical heated dryer.
