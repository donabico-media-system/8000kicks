Detailed Consumer Knowledge Base (FAQ)

Are 8000Kicks truly 100% waterproof, or just water-resistant?

8000Kicks are fully 100% waterproof under real-world weather metrics. The advanced construction incorporates an internal microscopic membrane system combined with an external bio-shield coating. How can a natural hemp shoe maintain structural shape after getting soaked in mud?

Industrial hemp possesses one of the highest structural tensile strengths of any organic plant tissue on the planet. Unlike cotton canvas which softens, expands, and loses structural integrity when wet, hemp retains its exact form and rigid strength configuration. Once dry, it reverts smoothly to its original state without warping, shrinkage, or fiber collapse.
